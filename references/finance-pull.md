# Finance and AI Native traction template

This public file is a configurable finance pull, not a company forecast. It contains no internal target, document link, token, or current-period deadline. Replace the placeholders with the latest approved finance/BP requirements for the organization under review. If the approved finance source is unavailable, mark the affected fields `GAP` and stay in preliminary mode.

## Minimum baseline pack

For the current planning cycle, collect comparable:

- prior full-year actual;
- prior second-half actual;
- current first-half actual or latest forecast;
- current second-half budget;
- current full-year forecast;
- source, owner, definition, and comparable basis for every number.

Use the latest approved SP/BP periods from the source pack, not dates hard-coded in this skill.

## Metric definitions and reconciliation

At minimum separate:

- business/circle revenue from regional or total-market revenue;
- sell-in (SI), sell-out (SO), recognized revenue, bookings, and backlog;
- gross profit, contribution margin, operating profit, net profit, and cash;
- unit efficiency and controllable operating expense;
- AI package/model/API/software/data/implementation cost from human cost without double counting.

If the same claim has multiple definitions or values, show them side by side and pause the affected conclusion until the named finance authority resolves it. A shipment, order, channel commitment, or SI is a leading indicator—not a business result—until SO, collection, margin, quality, trust, and delivery are verified.

## Full-cost economics

For low-price, private-label, ODM, channel-specific, or other boundary-sensitive proposals, include:

- product and tooling cost;
- dedicated people and shared-capability cost;
- quality/testing, warranty, service, returns, and rework;
- inventory, collection, working capital, and financing exposure;
- marketing, channel incentives, and partner fees;
- brand externality, cannibalization, and opportunity cost.

A positive narrow marginal contribution is not sufficient for `通过`.

## Causal bridge

Every material target must show the bridge from initiative to:

1. revenue or downstream value;
2. healthy gross margin and operating result;
3. cash, inventory, and collection;
4. unit efficiency and capacity;
5. trust, quality, and delivery;
6. actual AI workflow adoption and measurable leverage, where AI spend is claimed.

Treat targets as `JUDGMENT` until the baseline and owner confirm them. Treat unverified current-performance claims as `GAP`.

## Staged release rules

- name the current validation budget, release condition, stop condition, owner, and review date;
- do not approve irreversible investment before the organization’s E3-equivalent evidence gate;
- time-box third-curve or incubation work with an option ceiling and expiry/kill date;
- make every budget line traceable to an SP opportunity, a BP milestone, and the evidence it buys.

## Finance review rules

- never treat an aggressive target as a strategy;
- if a baseline is missing, write `待补数据`/`GAP`; do not estimate silently;
- if definitions conflict, preserve both definitions and stop the affected conclusion;
- evaluate AI spend through adopted workflows and measurable business leverage, not procurement or tool counts;
- require a named finance authority and review date for unresolved financial definitions.
